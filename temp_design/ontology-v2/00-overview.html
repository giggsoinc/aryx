1The north star — why an ontology at all

Agent accuracy is not a model problem, it's a structure problem. The ontology is the substrate that turns a guessing agent into a citing one. This is the chain every screen must serve.

The premise, plainly

"A business person who knows nothing about ontology — job on the line — must ship a model good enough that AI agents answer accurately over it. Nail that, and Aryx is the next Palantir in the making. Aryx's job is to make that person succeed."

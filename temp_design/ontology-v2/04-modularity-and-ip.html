3Above the line — the Aryx Engine (the IP)

Seven thick components no substrate gives you. This is where ontology engineering actually happens — the work a knowledge-engineering team would do by hand over months, automated.

The Aryx Ontology Engine

proprietary · never an adapter
1

Ontology Inference

Schema + samples + docs → classes, subsumption (is Customer ⊑ Party?), object-vs-data properties, relationship inference with cardinality. Multi-signal ML + LLM + statistics.

2

Entity Resolution + Survivorship

Band-routed funnel dedupes 200M dirty rows into golden entities + golden records. Decades of MDM, compressed into AI. A graph DB does not do this.

3

Axiom Proposal

Infers OWL2 constraints from evidence — Device ⊓ Customer = ∅. The reasoner only checks axioms; Aryx proposes them. That's the engineering.

4

Confidence · Provenance · Verification

Every node/edge/axiom/answer scored from evidence, traced to source.column, citation-verified. The agentic-accuracy engine.

5

Domain Funnel

Partitions a 200-source estate into domain ontologies; routes a question to its domain subgraph + only its feeding sources. Accuracy + cost at scale.

6

HITL Learning Loop

Every human accept/reject is labeled data; inference improves per-domain, forever. The compounding flywheel — three years of a customer's approvals can't be cloned.

7

Deliberation Adjudicator (the Andie pattern)

For OUTLIERS only: a multi-voice panel — Proposer · Skeptic · Domain · Governance · Judge — debates the call, yields a vote-derived confidence + recorded rationale (audit-grade).

▸ gated by an outlier detector: signal-disagreement · knife-edge · blast-radius · novelty · governance-sensitive → fires on ~1%. A scalpel, not a census.
The boundary rule: the Engine's logic — prompts, ER strategy, survivorship, axiom inference, the deliberation harness, the labeled-data loop — lives in the CORE, behind the ports. It never leaks into a swappable adapter. The day the clever bit lands in the Postgres adapter is the day you've given it to Oracle for free.
